# 流感疫苗信息 | Influenza Vaccine Information

## 为什么接种流感疫苗 | Why Vaccinate Against Flu

流感疫苗是预防流感最有效的方法：
- 每年接种可降低流感感染风险50-60%（在疫苗株与流行株匹配良好时）
- 即使感染，接种者的症状通常更轻，住院和死亡风险更低
- 群体免疫效益：当班级接种率超过70%，可显著减少班级内传播

Annual flu vaccination reduces infection risk by 50–60% when vaccine strains match circulating strains. Even with breakthrough infection, vaccinated individuals have milder illness and lower hospitalization risk. Herd effect: class vaccination rates >70% significantly reduce within-class transmission.

## 中国流感疫苗接种建议 | China Flu Vaccination Recommendations

根据《中国流感疫苗预防接种技术指南（2023-2024）》：

**优先接种人群（中国CDC推荐）:**
1. 6月龄至5岁儿童（最高风险人群）
2. 60岁及以上老年人
3. 慢性病患者（心脏病、糖尿病、哮喘等）
4. 医务人员
5. 6月龄以下婴儿的家庭成员（提供保护屏障）
6. 孕妇

**中小学生建议:**
- 强烈推荐每年接种
- 部分省市（如北京）已将6-14岁儿童纳入免费接种计划
- 沈阳地区：请咨询当地社区卫生服务中心

Per China CDC guidelines (2023–2024), priority groups include: children 6 months to 5 years, adults ≥60, those with chronic conditions, healthcare workers, household contacts of infants <6 months, and pregnant women. Annual vaccination is strongly recommended for school-age children.

## 接种时间 | Vaccination Timing

- **最佳接种时间**：每年9月至10月（北方流感季节前）
- **最迟接种时间**：整个流行季均可，越早越好
- 接种2周后抗体水平达到保护性水平
- 保护效力维持约6-8个月

Best time to vaccinate: September–October, before flu season begins in northern China. Protection develops within 2 weeks and lasts approximately 6–8 months. Vaccination is still beneficial even after flu season starts.

## 疫苗类型 | Vaccine Types Available in China

**三价灭活疫苗（IIV3）:**
- 覆盖：甲型H1N1、甲型H3N2、乙型Yamagata/Victoria系
- 可用于≥6月龄人群
- 肌肉注射（大腿外侧或上臂三角肌）

**四价灭活疫苗（IIV4）:**
- 在三价基础上增加一个乙型系
- 推荐优先使用
- 可用于≥3岁人群

**鼻喷流感疫苗（LAIV）:**
- 适用范围：3-17岁儿童
- 不需要注射，接受度高
- 不适用于：免疫缺陷者、哮喘、使用水杨酸类药物者

Quadrivalent inactivated vaccine (IIV4) is preferred and available for children ≥3 years. Live attenuated intranasal vaccine (LAIV) is available for ages 3–17 and is needle-free (higher acceptance), but contraindicated in immunocompromised patients, those with asthma, or aspirin users.

## 常见疑问解答 | Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: 接种后会得流感吗？**
A: 不会。灭活疫苗不含活病毒，不会引起流感。部分人接种后有轻微手臂酸痛、低烧，属正常免疫反应，1-2天内消退。

Vaccine cannot cause flu — it contains inactivated virus. Mild arm soreness or low-grade fever after vaccination is a normal immune response and resolves within 1–2 days.

**Q: 每年都需要接种吗？**
A: 是。流感病毒每年发生变异（抗原漂移），疫苗株每年更新以匹配预期流行毒株。前一年的抗体保护率会随时间下降，因此建议每年重新接种。

Yes, annual vaccination is necessary because influenza viruses mutate (antigenic drift) and vaccine strains are updated each year to match predicted circulating strains. Prior year antibodies wane and are not strain-matched.

**Q: 对鸡蛋过敏的孩子能接种吗？**
A: 轻度鸡蛋过敏者通常可接种（在医疗监督下）；严重鸡蛋过敏者应咨询医生，可使用无蛋细胞培养疫苗（中国部分地区已上市）。

Children with mild egg allergy can usually be vaccinated under medical supervision. Those with severe egg allergy should consult a physician; egg-free cell-based vaccines are available in some regions of China.

## 学校集体接种组织建议 | School Vaccination Coordination

学校可与当地卫生机构合作开展集体接种：
1. 提前告知家长，收集知情同意书
2. 安排接种日（通常在当地疾控/社区卫生中心协助下进行）
3. 接种后留观15-30分钟
4. 记录接种率（目标：≥70%）
5. 班级接种率纳入流感风险评估指标

Schools can coordinate with local health authorities for group vaccination: obtain parental consent, schedule vaccination day with CDC support, observe students for 15–30 minutes post-vaccination, record vaccination rates (target ≥70%), and use vaccination rates as a risk factor in flu monitoring.
