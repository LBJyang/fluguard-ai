# 校园流感防控措施 | School-Based Flu Prevention

## 中国学校卫生工作条例要求 | China School Health Regulations

根据《学校卫生工作条例》（2019年修订版）及教育部相关文件，学校在流感高发季节应：

1. 每日开展晨检（morning health check），由班主任或卫生老师在上午第一节课前完成
2. 发现发烧（体温≥37.5°C）、咳嗽、咽痛学生，应及时通知家长接回
3. 当班级出现3例及以上流感样病例时，应向学校卫生室报告，必要时报告当地疾控中心
4. 当全校流感样病例率达到5%时，学校卫生主管部门可决定停课

Per China's School Health Work Regulations, schools must: conduct daily morning health checks, isolate students with fever ≥37.5°C, report cluster cases (≥3 in one class) to the school health office, and consider class suspension if school-wide flu-like illness rate reaches 5%.

## 教室通风标准 | Classroom Ventilation Standards

《中小学校设计规范》（GB 50099-2011）规定：

- 普通教室换气次数：≥3次/小时
- 推荐每日开窗通风≥3次，每次≥15分钟
- 室内CO2浓度控制目标：<1000 ppm（优）；<1500 ppm（良）；>1500 ppm需立即通风
- 冬季可采用对流通风（开对角窗）减少冷风直接吹学生

Chinese classroom design standard requires ≥3 air changes/hour. Open windows ≥3 times daily for ≥15 minutes each. Target indoor CO2 below 1000 ppm. In winter, use cross-ventilation (open diagonal windows) to avoid cold drafts.

## 晨检操作规程 | Morning Health Check Protocol

**步骤 Steps:**

1. **问**：询问学生昨晚及今早的体温、有无发烧、咳嗽、乏力
2. **看**：观察学生面色、精神状态，有无流涕、眼红
3. **量**（选做）：对可疑学生测量体温
4. **记**：填写晨检记录表，标注异常学生
5. **报**：发现问题及时通知卫生老师和家长

Ask about overnight temperature and symptoms. Visually inspect for signs of illness. Optionally measure temperature for suspects. Record findings. Report to school health nurse and notify parents of symptomatic students.

## 消毒规程 | Disinfection Protocol

**日常清洁消毒 Routine:**
- 教室地面、桌面：每日用含氯消毒液（500 mg/L有效氯）拖擦一次
- 门把手、开关、水龙头：每日擦拭消毒2次
- 键盘、鼠标：每日酒精擦拭

**流行期强化 Enhanced during outbreak:**
- 每日消毒频次加倍
- 开窗通风同时用紫外线灯照射教室（注意人不在场时使用）
- 校内公共区域增加消毒站点

Use 500 mg/L chlorine solution for floors and desks daily. Wipe high-touch surfaces (handles, switches) twice daily. During outbreaks, double cleaning frequency and use UV light for unoccupied rooms.

## 空间布局风险评估 | Spatial Risk Assessment

学校建筑空间布局影响流感传播风险：

- **楼梯口/电梯口**：人流量大、接触频繁，是高风险节点
- **低楼层教室**：通风相对较差，需加强开窗频率
- **图书馆/体育馆**：大型聚集场所，流行期应限制使用或强化通风
- **走廊/过道**：接触传播高风险区，应保持清洁

High-risk spatial nodes: stairwells and elevator landings (high foot traffic), lower-floor classrooms (poorer ventilation), libraries and gyms (large gatherings), and hallways (contact transmission). Implement targeted interventions at these locations first.

## 班级应急响应分级 | Class-Level Emergency Response Levels

| 风险等级 Risk Level | 触发条件 Trigger | 应对措施 Response |
|---|---|---|
| 低 LOW | 24h咳嗽<50次 | 常规晨检，加强通风 |
| 中 MEDIUM | 24h咳嗽50-100次 或 2例发烧 | 加强晨检，通知家长注意观察，全班消毒 |
| 高 HIGH | 24h咳嗽>100次 或 ≥3例发烧 | 上报卫生室，考虑停课，全面消毒，家长告知书 |

Low risk (<50 coughs/24h): routine checks, improved ventilation. Medium risk (50–100 coughs or 2 fever cases): enhanced checks, parent notification. High risk (>100 coughs or ≥3 fever cases): report to health office, consider class suspension, full disinfection.

## 个人防护建议 | Personal Protection Recommendations

**学生 Students:**
- 每天上学前量体温；有症状不上学
- 勤洗手（饭前便后、咳嗽后、触摸公共物品后）
- 咳嗽/打喷嚏用纸巾或肘部遮掩
- 不与他人共用水杯、餐具
- 每年接种流感疫苗（推荐在每年10月前完成）

**教师 Teachers:**
- 了解班级学生的症状变化
- 适时开窗通风，不因寒冷天气省略通风
- 咳嗽症状明显时佩戴口罩（外科或N95）

Students: check temperature daily before school; don't attend when symptomatic; wash hands frequently; cover coughs with tissue or elbow; don't share cups or utensils; get annual flu vaccine by October. Teachers: monitor student symptoms; ventilate regularly regardless of cold weather; wear mask if symptomatic.
