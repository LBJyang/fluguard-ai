# 流感基础知识 | Influenza Basics

## 什么是流感 | What is Influenza

流感（influenza）是由流感病毒引起的急性呼吸道传染病，主要分为甲型（A型）、乙型（B型）、丙型（C型）三大类。在中国北方，流感高峰期通常出现在每年11月至次年3月，儿童和老人是高危人群。

Influenza is an acute respiratory infectious disease caused by influenza viruses. The main types are Influenza A, B, and C. In northern China, the peak season typically runs from November to March. Children and the elderly are high-risk groups.

## 主要传播途径 | Main Transmission Routes

流感通过以下途径传播：

1. **飞沫传播（主要）**：感染者咳嗽、打喷嚏或说话时产生的飞沫，可直接感染附近人员（距离1-2米内）。
2. **接触传播**：手接触被病毒污染的物体表面后再触摸口、眼、鼻。
3. **气溶胶传播（密闭空间）**：在通风不良的密闭环境中，气溶胶可较长时间悬浮于空气中。

Transmission routes:
1. **Droplet transmission (primary)**: Droplets from coughing, sneezing, or talking can infect people within 1–2 meters.
2. **Contact transmission**: Touching contaminated surfaces then touching the mouth, eyes, or nose.
3. **Aerosol transmission (enclosed spaces)**: In poorly ventilated areas, aerosolized particles may remain suspended.

## 潜伏期与传染期 | Incubation and Infectious Period

- **潜伏期**：1–4天，平均2天
- **传染期**：成人在症状出现前1天至症状出现后5-7天；儿童传染期可能更长，长达10天
- **高峰传染期**：症状出现后第1-2天病毒量最大

Incubation period: 1–4 days (average 2 days). Infectious period: 1 day before symptoms through 5–7 days after onset in adults; up to 10 days in children. Peak viral shedding occurs on days 1–2 after symptom onset.

## 典型症状 | Typical Symptoms

流感的特点是急性起病，与普通感冒相比症状更重：

- 高烧（38-40°C），突然发生
- 全身肌肉酸痛、乏力
- 头痛
- 干咳
- 咽痛、鼻塞、流涕
- 部分儿童出现恶心、呕吐、腹泻

Flu symptoms are typically more severe than common cold and include: sudden high fever (38–40°C), severe body aches and fatigue, headache, dry cough, sore throat, nasal congestion. Some children may experience nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea.

## 与普通感冒的区别 | Flu vs. Common Cold

| 特征 Feature | 流感 Flu | 普通感冒 Common Cold |
|---|---|---|
| 发热 Fever | 高烧，突然 High, sudden | 少见 Rare |
| 全身症状 Systemic symptoms | 严重 Severe | 轻微 Mild |
| 咳嗽 Cough | 干咳，明显 Dry, prominent | 轻微 Mild |
| 传染性 Infectivity | 高 High | 中 Moderate |
| 发病速度 Onset | 急骤 Rapid | 渐进 Gradual |

## 高危并发症 | Serious Complications

儿童流感并发症风险更高，包括：
- 肺炎（细菌性或病毒性）
- 急性中耳炎
- 心肌炎
- 脑炎（罕见）

High-risk complications in children include: pneumonia (bacterial or viral), acute otitis media, myocarditis, and rarely encephalitis. Children under 5 are at highest risk for severe disease.

## 环境对病毒存活的影响 | Environmental Factors

- 低温（0-10°C）有利于流感病毒在气溶胶中存活
- 低湿度（相对湿度<50%）延长病毒在空气中的存活时间
- 室内CO2浓度>1000ppm通常提示通风不足，增加气溶胶传播风险
- 病毒在坚硬物体表面可存活24小时，在皮肤上约5分钟

Cold temperatures (0–10°C) and low humidity (<50% RH) prolong virus survival in aerosols. Indoor CO2 >1000 ppm indicates inadequate ventilation. Virus survives 24 hours on hard surfaces, ~5 minutes on skin.
