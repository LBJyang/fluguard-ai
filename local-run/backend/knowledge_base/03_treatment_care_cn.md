# 流感治疗与居家护理 | Flu Treatment and Home Care

## 何时就医 | When to Seek Medical Care

**以下情况应立即就医 Seek immediate medical attention:**

- 呼吸困难或呼吸急促
- 持续胸痛
- 嘴唇或面色发紫/发青
- 意识混乱、嗜睡、不易唤醒
- 发烧超过39°C且退烧药无效
- 婴幼儿（<2岁）出现发烧
- 症状好转后再次加重（警惕继发细菌感染）
- 有基础疾病（哮喘、心脏病、免疫缺陷等）

Seek emergency care for: difficulty breathing, persistent chest pain, cyanosis (blue lips/face), confusion or difficulty waking, fever >39°C unresponsive to antipyretics, any fever in infants <2 years, symptom relapse after initial improvement, or underlying conditions (asthma, heart disease, immunosuppression).

## 抗病毒药物 | Antiviral Medications

**奥司他韦（Oseltamivir / 达菲）**

- 流感特效抗病毒药，在症状出现**48小时内**使用效果最佳
- 儿童剂量（按体重）：<15kg:30mg/次；15-23kg:45mg/次；23-40kg:60mg/次；>40kg:75mg/次；每日2次，疗程5天
- 副作用：恶心、呕吐（随餐服用可减轻）
- 重要提示：需凭处方购买；不建议预防性自行使用

Oseltamivir (Tamiflu) is most effective when started within 48 hours of symptom onset. Dose for children: <15 kg: 30 mg twice daily; 15–23 kg: 45 mg twice daily; 23–40 kg: 60 mg twice daily; >40 kg: 75 mg twice daily. Course: 5 days. Take with food to reduce nausea. Requires prescription; not recommended for prophylaxis without medical guidance.

**中药辅助治疗 Traditional Chinese Medicine (adjunct):**

在轻症早期，以下中成药有一定辅助退热、缓解症状作用：
- 连花清瘟颗粒（清热解毒）：成人4粒×3次/日；儿童减半
- 板蓝根颗粒（清热利咽）：轻症辅助，证据有限

Note: Chinese herbal medicines like Lianhua Qingwen may help with mild symptom relief. Evidence is limited; they should not replace standard antiviral treatment for moderate-severe cases.

## 退烧处理 | Fever Management

**退烧药使用指南 Antipyretic guidelines:**

儿童首选对乙酰氨基酚（paracetamol / acetaminophen）：
- 剂量：10-15 mg/kg/次，每4-6小时一次，每日不超过4次
- 适用：体温≥38.5°C时使用
- 禁用：阿司匹林（儿童流感禁用，可能引起Reye综合征）
- 布洛芬可用于≥6月龄儿童：5-10 mg/kg/次

Preferred antipyretic for children: paracetamol (acetaminophen) 10–15 mg/kg per dose every 4–6 hours (max 4 doses/day) when temperature ≥38.5°C. Ibuprofen can be used for children ≥6 months (5–10 mg/kg/dose). **Do NOT use aspirin in children with flu** — risk of Reye's syndrome.

## 居家护理要点 | Home Care Essentials

**休息与隔离 Rest and Isolation:**
- 发烧期间卧床休息，症状消退后至少24小时才可返校
- 单独使用卫生间（如条件允许）
- 照顾者戴口罩；勤洗手

**补充水分 Hydration:**
- 大量饮水（>1000ml/日），以温水、米汤为佳
- 发烧时出汗多，注意补充电解质（淡盐水）
- 避免含糖饮料

**饮食 Diet:**
- 进食清淡易消化食物：稀饭、面条、蒸蛋
- 少量多餐
- 避免油腻、生冷食物

**环境 Environment:**
- 保持室内空气流通，温度维持在20-22°C
- 避免与家中其他成员近距离接触

Stay home and rest during fever; return to school only ≥24 hours after fever resolves without antipyretics. Isolate from household members when possible. Caregiver should wear mask and wash hands frequently. Drink plenty of warm fluids. Eat light, easy-to-digest foods. Keep room well-ventilated at 20–22°C.

## 返校标准 | Return-to-School Criteria

根据中国卫生健康委员会指南：

1. **体温正常满24小时**（未服退烧药情况下）
2. **咳嗽、流涕等症状明显缓解**
3. **不再具有传染性**（通常为发病后7天）

满足以上条件后方可返校。建议向学校提交医疗机构的诊断证明或康复证明。

Students may return to school when: (1) fever-free for ≥24 hours without antipyretics, (2) other symptoms (cough, runny nose) significantly improved, and (3) ≥7 days since symptom onset. A medical certificate may be required.

## 流感与普通感冒的居家护理区别 | Flu vs. Cold Home Care

流感症状更重，需要：
- 更长的隔离期（7天 vs. 3-5天）
- 更积极的退烧处理
- 更密切的病情观察（警惕并发症）
- 更多的卧床休息

Flu requires: longer isolation (7 days vs. 3–5 for cold), more aggressive fever management, closer monitoring for complications, and more complete bed rest.
